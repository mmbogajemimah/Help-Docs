Introduction Last updated: 2025-02-26

Welcome to the CMSME Club User Training Manual! This manual is designed to guide you through the functionalities and features of the CMSME Club Platform, a cutting-edge digital solution tailored to meet the legal needs of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Whether you are a business owner, legal professional, or an administrator managing the platform, this manual will provide you with the knowledge and tools necessary to navigate the platform efficiently and make the most of its comprehensive legal services.

About CMSME Club

CMSME Club is a revolutionary platform that aims to simplify and enhance the way SMEs access and engage with legal services. By leveraging advanced technology and customer-centric design, CMSME Club provides a seamless and integrated experience for users, offering a wide range of legal services, including legal consultations, document reviews, legal opinions, legal audits, and access to a vast library of legal documents and resources. The platform is designed to be accessible via both web-based and mobile-based interfaces, ensuring that users can access legal support anytime, anywhere.

The primary goal of CMSME Club is to empower SMEs by providing them with the legal tools and resources they need to operate within legal frameworks, mitigate risks, and ensure long-term business success. By offering affordable, flexible, and high-quality legal services, CMSME Club is setting a new standard in the legal services industry, making legal support more accessible and convenient for businesses of all sizes.

Purpose of the User Training Manual

This User Training Manual serves as a comprehensive guide to help you understand and utilize the CMSME Club Platform effectively. Whether you are a new user or an experienced administrator, this manual will walk you through the platform's key features, modules, and functionalities. It is structured to provide step-by-step instructions, best practices, and tips to ensure that you can navigate the platform with confidence and ease.

The manual is divided into sections that correspond to the different modules of the platform. Each section provides detailed explanations of the features available within that module, along with instructions on how to perform specific tasks, such as managing user accounts, booking consultations, requesting document reviews, and accessing legal resources.

Registration

Welcome to the SME Club registration page! Follow this guide to create your account step by step.

Registration Form Overview

The registration form includes several required fields (marked with a red asterisk *).

Input Fields

Below the logo, you will find the registration form with the following fields:

  • First Name* – Enter your first name.
  • Last Name* – Enter your last name.
  • Username* – Choose a unique username.
  • Email* – Provide a valid email for verification.
  • Phone Number* – Enter a valid phone number.
  • Password* – Create a secure password.
  • Confirm Password* – Re-enter your password.

Sign-Up Process

After filling out the form, click the Sign Up button:

  • If there are errors, prompts will guide you to correct them.
  • If successful, you'll be redirected to your dashboard or email verification page.

Login for Existing Users

At the bottom of the form, you'll see:

"Already have an account? Login here"

Best Practices for Registration

  • Ensure email and phone details are correct for recovery.
  • Use a strong password with letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Remember your username and password.

Email Verification

After signing up, a verification email will be sent. If not received, request a new one. Once verified, you can choose:

  • Organization Account: Invite members and collaborate.
  • Individual Account: A personal profile without organization affiliation.

Creating an Organization Account

To register an organization, provide:

  • Organization Name
  • Registration Number
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Physical Address
  • Business Certificate
  • PIN Certificate

Creating an Individual Account

Provide the following details:

  • Address
  • National ID
  • PIN Certificate

Uploading the Letter of Engagement

After creating an account, download, sign, and upload the Letter of Engagement to the CMSME Portal.

Account Verification

Once uploaded, your account will be reviewed. After validation, you'll receive a confirmation email granting access.

Logging into the CMSME Portal

To access the CMSME Portal, users must enter their username and password used during registration. If a user forgets their password, they can reset it by clicking "Forgot Your Password?" and following the instructions to receive a reset link via email.

1. Accessing the Login Page

To log in, visit the SME Club login page, where you will find:

  • Username or Email Field – Enter your registered username or email.
  • Password Field – Enter your password.
  • Login Button – Click to access your account.
  • Forgot Your Password? – Click to reset your password if needed.
  • Sign Up Link – Click to register if you don’t have an account.

2. Logging Into Your Account

  • Enter your Username or Email in the first input field.
  • Enter your Password in the second input field.
  • Click the Login button.
  • If credentials are correct, you’ll be redirected to your dashboard.
  • If incorrect, an error message will appear. Ensure your details are correct and try again.

3. Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password, follow these steps:

  • Click on the Login Page "Forgot Your Password?"
  • Enter your registered email in the provided field.
  • Click "Reset Password" to receive an email with instructions.
  • Open your email and follow the steps to create a new password.
  • Use the new password to log in.

4. Creating a New Account

If you don’t have an account:

  • Click "Sign up here" on the login page.
  • Fill in the required details on the registration form.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the sign-up process.
  • After registration, return to the login page and sign in.

5. Troubleshooting Login Issues

  • ✅ Ensure your username or email is entered correctly.
  • ✅ Check if Caps Lock is enabled when entering your password.
  • ✅ Reset your password if you cannot remember it.
  • ✅ If issues persist, contact SME Club Support for assistance.

Forgot Password

If you need to reset your password, follow these steps:

  • Enter your new password in the designated field.
  • Re-enter the new password to confirm it.
  • Click Submit to save the changes.
  • Once updated, you will be prompted to log in using your new password.
