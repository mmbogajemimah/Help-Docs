User Guide
This manual is designed to guide you through the functionalities and features of the CMSME Club Platform, a cutting-edge digital solution tailored to meet the legal needs of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).
Registration
To join the CMSME Portal, users register, verify their email, select an account type, submit required details, and upload a signed Letter of Engagement for approval.
Dashboard
The SME Club Dashboard offers easy navigation to key features like documents, consultations, and invoices. It includes a search bar, user profile settings, and activity summaries. Users can subscribe to plans with legal consultations and document reviews by selecting a package and following the prompts.
Articles
The SME Club system enables users to manage blogs, webinars, and events, while admins handle creation, editing, and scheduling.
Documents
The SME Club Documents section enables users to search, manage, and download legal documents, while admins can edit, delete, and organize them efficiently.
Doc Reviews
The SME Club Document Reviews section enables users to submit, track, and manage reviews efficiently, displaying key details like focus area, status, and reviewer.
Health Scan
The SME Club Health Scan helps users assess business compliance by submitting and tracking requests with key details like focus area, status, and reviewer.
Consultations
The SME Club Consultations section enables users to schedule, track, and manage legal appointments with filtering, search, and navigation features.
Subscriptions
The SME Club Subscription section enables users to choose, purchase, and manage plans, track billing history, and upgrade subscriptions through a structured interface displaying plan options, payments, and invoices.
Invoices
The SME Club Invoice section enables users to track, manage, and download invoices with search, filter, and navigation tools. A structured table displays invoice ID, date, due date, status, and amount.
Receipts
The SME Club Receipt section enables users to track, manage, and download receipts. A structured table shows receipt ID, transaction details, amount, and date, with search, filter, and download options.

Youtube Tutorlals
CM SME YouTube Tutorials offer step-by-step guides on using the platform, covering user registration, company management, document handling, and financial transactions to help users navigate efficiently.
FAQs
FAQs provide quick answers to common questions, helping users find solutions without direct support. The SME Club Documentation aims to cover platform usage, features, and troubleshooting.
